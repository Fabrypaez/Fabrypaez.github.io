Technique

Augmented reality is used to either visually change natural environments in some way or to provide additional information to users. The primary benefit of AR is that it manages to blend digital and three-dimensional (3D) components with an individual's perception of the real world. AR has a variety of uses, from helping in decision-making to entertainment.

Project Development

The creation process of AR started with selecting the places and taking photographs of the area to interact. After that, we design the 3D and 2D assets and their animation. With Adobe Aero is easy to place everything; we select the area, put all the elements, and then export the file. This work is a prototype that I made selected some places in Galway city.
