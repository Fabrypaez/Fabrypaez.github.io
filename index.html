ABOUT THE FESTIVAL

The 1st edition of the Fabry Ego Film Festival is organised by Eilux Digital Art and directed by Fabry Páez. It will take place in two beautiful cities Galway, Ireland and San Luis Potosí, Mexico. This year we have the participation of 2 historical venues from both countries, with a similar architecture and a billboard of unique movies.

The Festival aims to inspire and rock the world to the rhythm of four selected categories by highlighting these sectors' creative dynamism and richness. This first edition will present films that have revolutionised and influenced the film industry in science fiction, fantasy, drama, and romance.

The Festival will be held in 2022 from 10th January until the 15th of January in both cities simultaneously.

THE VENUES

PALAS CINEMA

Pálás is a three screen arthouse cinema and cultural space in the UNESCO city of film- Galway.

Designed by Architect Tom de Paor, Pálás is a concrete monolith, five storey over double-height basement housing a three-screen independent cinema, a beautiful bar and terrace for you to enjoy with your fellow film-lovers. It occupies the garden of a reinstated 1820s merchant house.

Throughout the building you will discover 22 beautiful resin-coated windows designed by Irish artist Patrick Scott.

CINETECA ALAMEDA

The Cineteca Alameda is a cinema located in the city of San Luis Potosí, in the state of the same name, in Mexico . It is dedicated to the exhibition of films Mexico and other countries are housed in the Alameda Theater, an enclosure built in the 40s against the Alameda Juan Sarabia in that city.

Concerts of different musical genres are also held in this venue.
